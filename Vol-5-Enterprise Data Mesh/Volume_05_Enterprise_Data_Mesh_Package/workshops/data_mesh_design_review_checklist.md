# Data Mesh Design Review Checklist

## Governance
- Is every data product assigned to a domain owner and data steward?
- Is the product contract versioned and approved?
- Are quality thresholds measurable and monitored?

## Security
- Is the classification recorded in Knowledge Catalog?
- Are IAM, row policies, column policies and masking configured?
- Are restricted attributes separated or protected by policy tags?

## Operations
- Are SLOs defined for freshness, availability, correctness and support?
- Are dashboards and alerts configured before publication?
- Is cost allocation tagged to domain and product?
