# Review Checklists

## Architecture Review Board
- Requirements map to architecture components
- Alternatives and tradeoffs are documented
- Diagrams show trust boundaries and data flows
- Operational and cost models are defined

## Security Review Board
- IAM follows least privilege
- VPC Service Controls and network paths are validated
- CMEK and secret controls are documented
- Audit logs are routed and retained

## Production Readiness
- SLOs, alerts and runbooks are tested
- Backfill and replay procedures are documented
- DR procedures are tested
- Cost labels and dashboards are live
