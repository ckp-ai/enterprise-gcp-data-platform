# Architecture Foundation Workshop

## Objectives
- Confirm enterprise assumptions and target outcomes
- Validate business capabilities and priority domains
- Confirm landing-zone, security, governance and data-platform scope
- Identify executive decisions and open risks

## Agenda
1. Executive context and success criteria
2. Current-state pain points and data estate review
3. Target-state architecture walkthrough
4. Security and compliance constraints
5. Data domain and data-product discovery
6. MVP scope and roadmap
7. Decisions, risks and next actions

## Outputs
- Confirmed assumptions
- Prioritized domains
- MVP backlog
- ADR updates
- Risk register updates
